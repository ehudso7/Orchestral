Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 6, 2024

Our Commitment: At Orchestral, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address and password
  • Full name and company name (optional)
  • Billing information for paid plans
  • API keys and authentication tokens

1.2 Usage Data

  • API request metadata (timestamps, models used, token counts)
  • Performance metrics (latency, error rates)
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Dashboard interactions

1.3 Technical Information

  • IP addresses for security and rate limiting
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Device identifiers

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Delivery

  • Process API requests and route to appropriate AI models
  • Manage authentication and authorization
  • Track usage for billing purposes
  • Provide customer support

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to optimize performance
  • Debug issues and improve reliability
  • Develop new features based on usage data
  • Conduct research with anonymized data

2.3 Communication

  • Send service updates and notifications
  • Notify about security incidents
  • Share product announcements (with opt-out)
  • Respond to support requests

3. Data Storage and Security

Security First: We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3
  • Sensitive data is encrypted at rest using AES-256
  • API keys are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Strict access controls and authentication
  • Data centers comply with SOC 2 Type II standards

4. API Content and AI Models

Important: Content you send through our API is handled with special care.

  • API request content is NOT stored by default
  • Content is passed through to AI providers securely
  • Optional caching can be enabled with explicit consent
  • We do not use your content to train AI models
  • Content handling follows AI provider policies

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • AI Providers: Content forwarded for processing (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google)
  • Payment Processors: Billing information for payment processing (Stripe)
  • Service Providers: Infrastructure and analytics (AWS, Cloudflare)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or court order
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@orchestral.ai

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for authentication and security
  • Functional Cookies: Remember preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Understand usage patterns (can be disabled)
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We don't use advertising cookies

8. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while account is active
  • Usage logs: 90 days for operational purposes
  • Billing records: 7 years for legal compliance
  • Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days
  • Backups: Purged within 90 days of deletion

9. International Data Transfers

Our primary data centers are located in the United States. If you're accessing our Service from outside the US:

  • We use Standard Contractual Clauses for EU data transfers
  • We comply with GDPR requirements for EU residents
  • We respect data localization requirements where applicable

10. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising rights

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be notified via email or prominent notice on our Service. The "Last Updated" date indicates the latest revision.

13. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns:

  • Email: privacy@orchestral.ai
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@orchestral.ai
  • Address: 1234 Innovation Way, San Francisco, CA 94105
  • Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567

Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to transparency and will respond to all privacy inquiries within 72 hours.
